Three Dark Hosts

Lowri, Tomos, and Eira are the only survivors from their village. Left with a terrible curse, the siblings managed to create a new life for themselves in the port city of Dinas Môr. Six years later, the same cult that was responsible has started activity again and this time Lowri, Tomos, and Eira are determined to stop them before they can carry out their plans. However, it won't be easy when Lowri cannot hear anything about the cultists, Tomos cannot see any activities they do, and Eira is unable to speak about anything she hears or sees to the other two.


Age Rating: 16+

Content Warning: Body Horror

Status: Ongoing
